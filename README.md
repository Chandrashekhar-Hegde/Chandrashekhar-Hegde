## 💫 The Architect

I am an Applied-Research Engineer mapping the intersection of physical sensors, embedded systems, and machine learning. 

Currently serving as a **Project Research Scientist-3** at the Department of Public Health, JNMC (KAHER), I lead the technical development of an ICMR flagship project: a non-invasive multispectral-thermal probe for screening oral potentially malignant disorders. I don't just train AI models—I engineer the hardware pipeline, write the signal processing algorithms, build the physical prototypes, and deploy the entire architecture onto edge devices like the Raspberry Pi so it can run in district hospitals instead of isolated labs.

My background in mechanical engineering, advanced composites, and control systems drives my core philosophy: software is only as good as the physical hardware it commands. From conceptual physics to clinical deployment, I build systems that bridge the digital and physical worlds.
