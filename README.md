## 💫 About Me

Just another carbon-based life form trying to understand the universe through code, optics, and medical imaging. 

I am a **Medical Imaging Engineer** and **Project Research Scientist-3** at the Department of Public Health, JNMC (KAHER), working on an ICMR flagship project. I build point-of-care screening devices—from designing the optical acquisition stack and writing the spectral DSP, to training the classifier and deploying the entire pipeline on a Raspberry Pi so it can run in a district hospital instead of a lab. 

Before this, my focus was on mechanical engineering, advanced composites, and simulation. That physical engineering background is the reason I can build the hardware probe just as well as the AI model that reads it.

## 🚀 Cosmic Missions (Current & Past Projects)

### 🏥 Medical Imaging & Devices
* **`thermal-lesion-screening`:** Thermography classification with patient-level splits enforced in code, DICOM ingest, bootstrap CIs on every metric, and ONNX latency measured on the deployment board. Built because most of this literature reports sensitivity without specificity and splits by image instead of by patient.
* **`gait` (GaitOS):** Turns a $25 M5StickC into a clinical-grade inertial gait analysis system. Features a Madgwick quaternion filter, adaptive ZUPT, and on-device abnormality detection.
* **`raktasetu-app`:** Blood-donation platform for the Hubballi–Dharwad region featuring donor credits and a hospital staff console.

### 🔬 Imaging & Scientific Computing
* **`lpbf-optimizer`:** Physics-informed optimiser for laser powder bed fusion (LPBF).
* **`fiber_tracer_2.0`:** 3D fibre detection and tracing in reinforced polymer composites.
* **`market-intelligence-core`:** Deterministic market intelligence engine, built for performance in Rust.
